Lead & inbound automations

Never let a lead go cold — respond in 60 seconds, follow up for 14 days

The biggest revenue leak in service companies is slow lead response. Studies show responding within 5 minutes is 21× more effective than responding in 30 minutes. GHL's AI bot closes that gap permanently.

1

Inbound lead AI response bot

Trigger: form fill, website chat, missed call · responds in under 60 seconds · 24/7

Build first
TRIGGER: Website form filled OR missed call OR website chat initiated Instant
GHL AI Conversation Bot sends personalised SMS within 60 seconds — captures: building type, number of elevators, service needed, urgency level AI bot

If qualified standard lead → bot books a 15-minute discovery call directly into account manager's GHL calendar. Lead never waits for a human to call them back. Book call

SMS — sent within 60 seconds of form fill

"Hi [Name], this is Axxiom Elevator — thanks for reaching out! To get you to the right person fast, can you tell me: how many elevators does your building have, and is this a maintenance contract inquiry or an urgent repair? Reply here and we'll get back to you immediately."

The missed call text-back is the single highest-ROI automation in GHL for service companies. When someone calls Axxiom and nobody answers, GHL instantly sends an SMS: "Hi — we just missed your call. This is Axxiom Elevator. How can we help? Reply here and we'll get back to you right away." Converts 30–40% of missed calls that would otherwise go to a competitor.

Reputation automations

Build a 5-star reputation across every market — on autopilot

Axxiom needs Google reviews in every market. Right now they almost certainly have very few. GHL's review automation can change that in 90 days — turning every completed job into a potential 5-star review without anyone manually asking for one.

6

Post-job review request automation

Fires 48 hours after job complete · personalised SMS · 2-touch sequence

Build day one
TRIGGER: Job complete tag applied in GHL (from Dynamics webhook) + job type = PM or Repair + outcome = Pass/Resolved Qualified trigger
Wait 48 hours — gives client time to verify everything is working before asking for a review Wait
SMS Touch 1 (from local Axxiom number): "Hi [Name], this is [Account Manager] from Axxiom — [Tech Name] serviced your elevator at [Building] this week. If we did a great job, a quick Google review helps us more than you know. Only takes 30 seconds: [Direct Google Review Link]" SMS ask
IF reviewed → tag "Reviewed ✓" → stop sequence → trigger referral automation (Tab 4). IF not reviewed in 72 hours → send Email Touch 2 Branch logic
Email Touch 2 (72hrs after SMS): "Hi [Name], just following up on my text. I know your time is valuable — a 30-second review makes a huge difference for our small team serving [city]. [Review link] — thank you either way." Email follow

Key rule — never ask after an emergency or complaint

Add a condition: if job type = Emergency OR if a complaint tag exists → skip review sequence entirely. Asking for a review when a passenger was trapped earlier that day is the fastest way to get a 1-star response.

GHL pipeline setup

Two pipelines — new business + revenue upsells

GHL pipelines give Axxiom's account team complete visibility into where every prospect and upsell opportunity sits — and trigger automated actions at each stage move.

Pipeline 1 — New business (maintenance contracts)

Lead Captured→ AI bot fires, 14-day nurture starts
Discovery Call Booked→ Confirmation SMS + calendar reminder sequence
Site Visit Scheduled→ Pre-visit email with what to expect + tech bio
Proposal Sent→ 3-day follow-up SMS, 7-day email if no response
Proposal Follow-Up→ AI objection handling email ("We often hear X concern — here's how we address it")
Contract Signed→ Welcome sequence starts, Dynamics record created, account manager assigned task
Lost / Not Now→ Tagged by reason · enters 6-month re-engagement sequence

Pipeline 2 — Revenue upsells (existing clients)

Modernisation Signal Flagged→ Modernisation upsell sequence starts (Automation #8)
Conversation Started→ Account manager presents risk data + ROI model
Scope Under Discussion→ AI generates preliminary modernisation proposal from asset data
Proposal Sent→ 5-day follow-up SMS + financing options email
Project Signed→ Project kick-off email sequence · client communication plan starts
