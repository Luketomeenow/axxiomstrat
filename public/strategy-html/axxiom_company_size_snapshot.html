What the data tells us

Where Axxiom actually stands — synthesis from filings, M&A databases, and market data.

Very young, very fast-moving

Axxiom Elevator was formed in 2023 and is based in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, when private equity firm Gauge Capital acquired the business as a platform investment. Two years old with 8 acquisitions is exceptional velocity for a PE-backed rollup in any sector.

SEC filings

Substantial capital backing

Axxiom Elevator has raised $120M, backed by Gauge Capital. Gauge Capital is a leading middle-market private equity firm based in Southlake, Texas, managing more than $3.0 billion in capital. This is not a small regional operator — this is a well-capitalised growth platform with institutional resources.

Maven Group, LLCPrivSource

The latest acquisition signals ambition

The Ameritex acquisition is Axxiom's 8th acquisition and brings expansion opportunities in two new growing markets — Southwest Texas and the San Francisco Bay Area. They are simultaneously entering opposite coasts of the country, which tells you the acquisition pace is not slowing down.

ZoomInfo

Headcount — the honest answer

It is hard to pin down precisely. Official databases show small corporate headcount because most staff sit inside the acquired entities. The Liftech acquisition alone added over 90 new employees to the Axxiom family. Quality Elevator has operated for 43 years and carries significant headcount. Across 8 acquisitions in 7 states, a conservative estimate is 300–600 field technicians and support staff — but the real number is likely known only internally.

Bloomberg

Competitive context

Axxiom at 7 states in 2 years compares to American Elevator Group at 21 states in 5 years. Axxiom is moving faster per year of existence, but AEG has a significant head start in geographic coverage. The window to close that gap — while also building the AI and data foundation that AEG has not — is exactly what the strategy we've mapped out is designed to exploit.
