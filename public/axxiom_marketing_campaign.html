Campaign Strategy

The campaign operates on a two-tier architecture: Axxiom corporate builds category authority and trust, while each regional brand drives localized lead generation. Both tiers amplify each other.

Tier 1 — Corporate (Axxiom)

Category Authority + Network Story

Build Axxiom's reputation as the most trusted elevator network in the U.S. Focus on thought leadership, network expansion PR, partner acquisition, and LinkedIn authority. Audience: enterprise property groups, investors, potential partner companies.

Tier 2 — Regional Brands

Local Lead Generation + Vertical Marketing

Each brand runs geo-targeted Google PPC, local SEO, and vertical-specific outreach. Shared messaging framework, locally customized execution. Audience: local facility managers, property owners, building operators.

Core Campaign Themes
Theme 1

"Reliability In Motion"

Everything reinforces Axxiom's core positioning. Every elevator runs. Every call gets answered. Every technician shows up. Reliability is the product.

Theme 2

"Local Expertise, National Strength"

The 7-brand network is a feature, not a complexity. You get a technician who knows your building codes AND the backing of a national organization.

Theme 3

"Your Elevator Is The Last Thing You Should Think About"

Reframe the value prop from "elevator service" to "peace of mind." Target the mental load of property managers and facility directors.

Theme 4 — Urgency

"Every Hour of Downtime Has a Price"

Quantify the cost of an out-of-service elevator — tenant dissatisfaction, ADA violations, lost business. Make inaction feel expensive.

Theme 5 — Trust

"We Only Recommend What's Right for You"

Integrity as a brand pillar. Counter the "overselling" reputation of large OEMs. Let technicians be the heroes, not salespeople.

Theme 6 — Growth

"Expanding Into Your Community"

Turn network expansion into a PR story. New market entries are news. Use this to build awareness in pre-launch markets before the brand arrives.

90-Day Launch Roadmap

A phased execution plan that builds foundation first, activates paid channels second, and optimizes for scale in the third phase. Designed for a central marketing team coordinating with regional brands.

Days 1–15 · Foundation
Brand Audit & Asset Creation
Audit all 7 brand websites for SEO gaps, conversion rate issues, and messaging consistency. Create shared brand asset library (ad templates, email headers, social graphics). Build master keyword list per brand and market. Set up Google Analytics 4, Google Tag Manager, and call tracking across all brands.
Days 15–30 · Foundation
Local SEO Blitz
Fully optimize Google Business Profiles for all brands. Ensure consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across all directories. Build 3–5 city-specific landing pages per brand. Set up review generation workflow — automated post-service email requesting Google review.
Days 30–45 · Activation
Paid Media Launch
Launch Google Search campaigns for each brand (Emergency, Maintenance, Modernization). Set up Google Display retargeting. Launch LinkedIn Ads (Axxiom corporate) targeting facility managers. Install conversion tracking — form fills, calls, chat initiations all tracked as conversions.
Days 45–60 · Activation
Content + Email Engine
Publish first 4 blog posts across Axxiom and top regional brands. Launch email newsletter to existing client list. Deploy 3-email lead nurture sequence for new consultation requests. Begin weekly LinkedIn publishing cadence (Axxiom corporate page + exec profiles).
Days 60–75 · Optimization
Data Review & Optimization Sprint
Review PPC performance: cut underperforming keywords, double down on top converters. A/B test ad headlines and landing page CTAs. Identify top-performing content and create 2 variations. Begin outreach to 3 property management companies per brand for referral partnership.
Days 75–90 · Scale
Scale & Systematize
Expand PPC to cover competitor conquest keywords. Launch vertical-specific campaigns (Healthcare, Hotel, Multifamily). Create playbook for new brand onboarding — so any future acquisition can be marketing-ready in 30 days. Set Q2 targets and present full campaign performance report.
